We are thrilled to unveil the newly redesigned Oswayo Valley School District website! This update represents our commitment to providing our community with better access to information and resources.

## What's New

- **Modern Design**: A fresh, clean interface that works beautifully on all devices
- **Easier Navigation**: Find what you need quickly with our improved menu structure
- **News & Updates**: Stay informed with our new news feed featuring the latest district announcements
- **Mobile Friendly**: Access information on the go with our fully responsive design

## Stay Connected

We encourage you to explore the new site and discover all the resources available to students, parents, and community members. If you have any questions or feedback, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Thank you for being part of the Oswayo Valley School District community!
